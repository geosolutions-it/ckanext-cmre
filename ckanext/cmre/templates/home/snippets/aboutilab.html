The Information Laboratories Office (iLab) supports the execution of the Programme of Work of STO-CMRE through the conception, design, development and construction of state of the art technologies in computing, networking and modelling & simulation (M&S), ensuring interoperability, security and compliance with relevant policies and regulations.

The core of iLab's mission is the support to the "information supply chain" through which streams of heterogeneous data (ranging from in situ, to remote sensing and other sources) are transformed into added-value products, such as models, analyses and decision aids, to provide decision makers with the elements they require to take informed decisions.
